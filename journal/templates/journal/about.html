{% block title %}About AbstractNg{% endblock %}

Abstractng is an online archive of undergraduate and postgraduate theses, as well as scholarly papers from inaugural lectures, conferences and workshops.

Abstractng showcases the intellectual work of authors/individuals to a wide audience and creates opportunities for research collaboration as well as mentorship.

Reviews in abstractng are contributions to an abstract or a paper with the aim of increasing understanding of the topic or subject been covered. These contributions can be in the form of additional information, questions/seeking clarifications on grey areas, references etc.

You can contribute to the body of knowledge by adding that abstract/paper on abstractng.
